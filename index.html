Explore Sajek Valley

The Queen Of Hill And Roof Of Rangamati

Author: Masuma

Sajek valley is famous for its natural beauty. Sajek valley is known as the Queen of Hills & Roof of Rangamati. The valley is surrounded by mountains, dense forest, grasslands hilly tracks. Many small rivers flow through the mountains among which Kachalong and Machalong notable.
